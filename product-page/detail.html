Overview

Escape the hectic pace of urban life and get a taste of slow-living in gorgeous mud cottages at Tripoto’s Mindful Retreat to Kasauli. Treat your senses with the views of pristine mountains, waterfalls, and rejuvenating walks through pine forests, alive with birdsongs. Break away from the monotony of your urban life by exploring Himachal culture, savouring delicious local food, stargazing, and enjoying movies in the open air as the sun sets. End your evening with a fun karaoke session with panoramic views on the rooftop cafe. Come, and join us for a nostalgic trip to your favourite hill station, just a few hours away from Delhi NCR.

How to reach:

You can find A Kasauli Ggaon by Echor, Kasauli on Google Maps

  • By Road: A Kasauli Ggaon can be reached by road from Delhi (287 km) and Chandigarh (57 km).
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Chandigarh Airport in Chandigarh, 68 km away from the property. It takes about two hours from the airport to reach the property.
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Kalka railway station, at a distance of 28 km from the property.
  • By Bus: Kasauli bus stop (5.2 km) is in close proximity and is seventeen minutes away from the property.
